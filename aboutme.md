My name is Ryan. I built Mail a Picture to make it super easy for me to send pictures of my kids to my parents and an even easier way for my parents to send images to their grandkids while they travel.

In the summer of 2020, my parents took their new RV and traveled for months around the country and up into Alaska. They would search every town they drove into for postcards to send to their three grandkids. The kids would absolutely light up each time they came in the mail. As I would tell my Mom how the kids loved their postcards, she would complain how hard it is to find postcards and they wish they could send the pictures they were taking instead.

It was a light bulb moment for me and I soon started planning and building what would become Mail A Picture. My number one goal was to make it as easy as possible for my Mom and Dad to use the website and send pictures. I really hope I succeeded in that goal and that you will agree.

Please check out [mailapicture.com](https://mailapicture.com) and send some happiness to friends and family today!
